Our current broadcast lineup
King of Nordic
Every Friday at 19:00 CET
King of Nordic is the headline show that ESEN produces. The show is produced live from our studio in Stockholm, featuring CSGO commentators from around the world, calling the action between up-and-coming teams from the Nordic region.
the Aftershow
Every Friday at 22:30 CET
The Aftershow is a variety talkshow airing immediately following King of Nordic each friday, hosted by our very own Jesper "kreativ" Sedendahl.
the Teammates Show
Every Friday at 23:30 CET
The Teammates Show is the "Late Night" segment of the King of Nordic evening, featuring a downtempo mix of comedy, gameplay, and discussion.
